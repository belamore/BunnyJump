Bunny Jump

Art 259 project 2

By: Alyssa Fraser & Belen Morales

Bunny jump is a platformer game where you get to play as a pink bunny exploring the wilderness. Reach the end of the game by avoiding enemies and using mushrooms to get to high platforms while collecting as many carrots as you can!

Control your character by using the left and right arrow keys and jump by using the space bar!
